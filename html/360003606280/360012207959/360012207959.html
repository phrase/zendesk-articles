Integrations provide a way to move translation texts between Memsource accounts, Content Management Systems, marketing automation platforms, source code repositories, and other 3rd-party systems.

Connectors function based on the pull principle; content is pulled into Memsource for translation.

Plugins function based on the push principle; content is pushed into Memsource for translation.

Available Connectors and Plugins

Not every edition includes every connector. If a connector is required, upgrade your account or in the case of Enterprise accounts, purchase the connector.
