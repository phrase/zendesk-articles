Photoshop does not automatically update documents with their new translations. To update layers, do one of the following:

  • When opening the translated PSD file in Adobe Photoshop, confirm the dialog box asking for updates to certain layers.

  • If the dialog box does not show up or does not update all the translated content, click the layers containing the translated text to update them.
