Auto-complete does not support non-space languages (Japanese, Chinese, Korean).

Auto-complete suggestions are based on matches from TM, TB and MT (if applied to the job). If suggestions are available, a red line is presented under the word. Hover over the red line to see suggestions and click on a selected word to have it replace the original in the text. If no suggestions are valid, the word can be added to the dictionary.

Auto-complete is activated in the Tools menu or in the Misc tab.
